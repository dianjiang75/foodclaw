## Test 1: Allergy Diet Filter -- FAIL (CRITICAL)

**Endpoint**: `/api/search?lat=40.7264&lng=-73.9878&diet=nut_free,gluten_free&limit=20`

**Result**: 20 dishes returned. 19/20 pass. 1 CRITICAL violation.

### CRITICAL VIOLATION: Nabeyaki Udon

- **Dish**: Nabeyaki Udon (Restaurant: Raku)
- **Description**: "Hot pot udon with shrimp tempura, chicken, egg, mushroom, and vegetables"
- **Flags**: nut_free=true, gluten_free=true, dietary_confidence=0.95
- **Problem**: Udon noodles are made from wheat flour. This dish is definitively NOT gluten-free. The evaluator's `KNOWN_ALLERGEN_DISHES.gluten_free` list includes both "udon" and "nabeyaki", BUT the dish still passes because confidence (0.95) exceeds the `WARNING_THRESHOLD` (0.90). The evaluator adds a warning ("This dish traditionally contains ingredients related to gluten free -- verify with the restaurant before ordering") but does NOT exclude the dish.
- **Root cause**: The evaluator applies `WARNING_THRESHOLD` (0.90) for known allergen dishes but does not hard-block them. A dish named "Nabeyaki Udon" with confidence 0.95 passes the threshold check and is only warned, not excluded.
- **Impact**: A celiac patient could order this dish relying on the gluten_free=true flag. The warning is present but the dish should not appear in gluten_free results at all.
- **Recommendation**: Known allergen dishes (from KNOWN_ALLERGEN_DISHES) should be hard-excluded from allergy-critical diet filter results regardless of confidence. The keyword safety net on lines 159-168 of the evaluator checks for "wheat" and "flour tortilla" in descriptions but does NOT check for "udon" -- the KNOWN_ALLERGEN_DISHES list is only used for threshold comparison, not for the keyword safety net. Add "udon", "ramen", "noodle", and other known gluten-containing words to the keyword safety net.

### Borderline Concerns (not violations but noteworthy)

- **Carciofi Fritti** (fried artichokes): Traditionally battered in wheat flour, but described as "Fried artichokes Roman-style with lemon and sea salt" -- no mention of batter/flour. Marked gluten_free=true at conf=0.89 with appropriate warning. The keyword net catches "flour" but the description omits it. Risk: moderate.
- **Falafel**: Traditionally contains flour as a binder, but chickpea-based versions can be gluten-free. Marked gluten_free=true at conf=0.85 (minimum threshold) with appropriate warning. Borderline acceptable.
